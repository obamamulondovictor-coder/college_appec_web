What We Offer

College Appec TSS Remera-Rukoma provides hands-on, industry-aligned technical and vocational education across four specialised trades. Our programmes are accredited by NESA and aligned with Rwanda's national skills development agenda, equipping students with practical competencies for the modern workforce.

SWD

Software Development

The Software Development programme trains students in modern programming languages, web technologies, database management, and software engineering principles. Graduates are equipped to design, build, and maintain software solutions for businesses and organisations.

Key Subjects

  • Web Design & Development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Programming Fundamentals (Python, Java)
  • Database Management (MySQL, PostgreSQL)
  • Software Engineering & Project Management
  • Mobile Application Development
  • Networking Basics & Cybersecurity Awareness

Career Opportunities

  • Web Developer / Front-end Developer
  • Software Engineer
  • Database Administrator
  • IT Support Specialist
  • Freelance Developer / Entrepreneur

BDC

Building & Construction

The Building & Construction programme provides students with comprehensive knowledge of construction techniques, architectural drawing, site management, and the use of modern building materials. Students gain practical skills through on-site projects and workshops.

Key Subjects

  • Architectural Drawing & Design
  • Masonry & Bricklaying
  • Carpentry & Joinery
  • Plumbing & Sanitation
  • Electrical Installation Basics
  • Construction Site Management & Safety

Career Opportunities

  • Construction Technician
  • Site Supervisor
  • Architectural Draughtsman
  • Building Inspector
  • Contractor / Entrepreneur

CSA

Computer System & Architecture

The Computer System & Architecture programme focuses on hardware fundamentals, operating systems, networking, and system administration. Students learn to assemble, configure, troubleshoot, and maintain computer systems and networks.

Key Subjects

  • Computer Hardware & Assembly
  • Operating Systems (Windows, Linux)
  • Computer Networking (LAN, WAN, TCP/IP)
  • System Administration & Maintenance
  • Cybersecurity Fundamentals
  • Cloud Computing Basics

Career Opportunities

  • IT Technician / Hardware Engineer
  • Network Administrator
  • System Administrator
  • Cybersecurity Analyst
  • IT Consultant
Apply Now →
ACC

Accounting & Finance

The Accounting programme equips students with financial literacy, bookkeeping, taxation, and business management skills. Students learn to manage financial records, prepare reports, and support business decision-making using modern accounting tools.

Key Subjects

  • Financial Accounting & Bookkeeping
  • Cost & Management Accounting
  • Taxation & Rwanda Revenue Authority (RRA) Compliance
  • Business Mathematics & Statistics
  • Computerised Accounting (QuickBooks, Sage)
  • Entrepreneurship & Business Management

Career Opportunities

  • Accountant / Bookkeeper
  • Finance Officer
  • Tax Consultant
  • Auditor
  • Business Owner / Entrepreneur
