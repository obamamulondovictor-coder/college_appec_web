Who We Are

College Appec TSS
Remera-Rukoma

College Appec Remera-Rukoma TSS is a Government-Aided Technical Secondary School located in Remera-Rukoma, Kamonyi District, Southern Province, Rwanda. Founded with a vision to bridge the skills gap in Rwanda's growing economy, the college provides hands-on, industry-aligned technical and vocational education.

Accredited by NESA (National Examination and School Inspection Authority) and aligned with Rwanda's Vision 2050 and the national skills development agenda, College Appec equips students with practical competencies that make them immediately employable or ready to start their own enterprises.

Our Journey

College Milestones

2019

Foundation

College Appec TSS Remera-Rukoma was officially established as a Government-Aided school under the Ministry of Education.

2020

First Intake

The college welcomed its first cohort of students across the SWD, BDC, CSA, and Accounting programmes.

2022

NESA Accreditation

All four trade programmes received full accreditation from NESA, validating the quality of education delivered.

2023

First Graduates

College Appec celebrated its first graduating class — over 60 students entered the workforce or pursued higher education.

2024

Infrastructure Growth

New computer labs, construction workshops, and accounting classrooms were commissioned, doubling the college's capacity.

2025+

Digital Transformation

Launch of the college's digital platform, online student portal, and partnerships with tech companies for internship placements.
